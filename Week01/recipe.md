# Chocolate Chip Pancakes

## Description
These chocolate chip pancakes are fluffy, sweet, and perfect when paired with maple syrup. They are excellent for a snow day!

---

## Ingredients
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour  
- 1 tablespoon sugar  
- 1 teaspoon baking powder  
- 1 cup milk  
- 1 egg  
- ½ cup chocolate chips  

---

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, **whisk** together the dry ingredients.
2. Add the milk and egg, and mix until just combined.
3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
4. Cook pancakes on a lightly greased pan until golden brown.
