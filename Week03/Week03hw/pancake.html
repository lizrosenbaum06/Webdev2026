    • Chocolate Chip Pancakes

    These chocolate chip pancakes are fluffy, sweet, and perfect when paired with maple syrup. They are excellent for a snow day!

    Ingredients

    • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    • 4 teaspoons baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoons salt
    • 2 tablespoons sugar
    • 1 1/4 cup milk
    • 1 egg beaten
    • 3 tablespoons butter melted
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
    • 1 cup chocolate chips

    Instructions

    1. Whisk together dry ingredients until combined.
    2. In another bowl whish together milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
    3. Whisk in wet ingredients into dry ingredients.
    4. Fold in chocolate chips.
    5. Heat skillet over medium-high heat.
    6. Scoop the batter onto the pan in 1/4 cup measurements.
    7. Cook on each side for about 3-4 minutes, until golden on both sides.
